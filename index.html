Elyft Compressor is a tool for compressing, resizing images and changing images extensions (for example transforming a jpg into a png after compression). What differentiates it from other online compression tools is that it is written entirely in Javascript, which means that you will be able in the near future (during the next update) to perform your image compression even while offline.Elyft Compressor uses one or more cores of your processors to compress your images very quickly.
